About Me

Dr. Ima Studd is a Psychiatrist and has been practicing since 2006, gaining vast experience throughout the field of Psychiatry, medication management, and psychotherapy. He has worked in emergency psychiatric centers and crisis services, on a consultation basis for medical hospitals, on both acute and long term inpatient treatment centers, as well as rehabilitation programs throughout the United States.
